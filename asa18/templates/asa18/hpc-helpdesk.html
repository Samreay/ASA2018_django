ADACS / OzSTAR / Pawsey Live HPC Helpdesk

Location: Conference Foyer (ATC Ground Level)
When: Wednesday/Thursday 10.30am-4.30pm

If you are attending the ASA meeting and would like an opportunity to speak in person to a high performance computing (HPC) expert about your data and/or computing needs then please visit our live helpdesk. Staff from the following organisations will be in attendance:

  • Astronomy Data and Computing Services (ADACS);
  • Swinburne OzSTAR supercomputer;
  • Pawsey Supercomputing Centre.

If you would like to pre-book a time please send an email to eresearch@astro.swin.edu.au (making sure to mention the topic you are interested in). Otherwise just drop by for a chat when you have a spare moment.
